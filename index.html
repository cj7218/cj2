Primary Balance Explained
Primary Balance = Overall Balance + Net Interest
Fiscal Impulse = Change in Primary Balance (YoY)
Why primary matters: Net interest is fixed by the existing debt stock — it can't be cut quickly. Primary balance reflects actual spending & taxing decisions. Italy has a primary surplus despite an overall deficit — fiscally sustainable.
